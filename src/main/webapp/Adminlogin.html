Services

A panchayat is not just a governing body; it is a platform for problem-solving and community development

Education

Panchayats can work towards ensuring access to quality education, promoting literacy programs, and improving school infrastructure and teaching standards.

Water Management

Panchayats can tackle water-related issues such as ensuring clean and accessible water supply, promoting rainwater harvesting, and managing water resources efficiently.

Healthcare

Panchayats can address healthcare challenges by promoting better healthcare facilities, organizing health camps, spreading awareness about hygiene and sanitation, and coordinating with government health programs.

Street light

This involves identifying areas with inadequate lighting, damaged lights, or areas that lack street lights altogether. Based on the assessment, the panchayat should develop a detailed plan to address the street light problems effectively.

Infrastructure Development

Panchayats can focus on improving the quality of roads, bridges, sanitation facilities, water supply systems, electricity, schools, and healthcare centers within the village.

Agriculture and Livelihoods

Panchayats can support farmers by providing agricultural infrastructure, training programs, access to credit, and promoting sustainable farming practices. They can also explore livelihood opportunities beyond agriculture, such as skill development and entrepreneurship.
